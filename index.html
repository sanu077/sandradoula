About Me

Welcome to Sandra Miranda's Baby Yoga & Doula Services

Hello! My name is Sandra Miranda, and I am a passionate advocate for mothers and babies. With years of experience in baby yoga and doula services, I am dedicated to supporting you on your journey to motherhood.

Baby Yoga is not only a fun and enjoyable experience for both you and your baby but also a beautiful way to enhance your bond. I believe in the power of nurturing touch and movement to create a loving connection with your little one.

As a certified doula, I am committed to providing compassionate care during the postpartum period. My goal is to offer emotional support, breastfeeding guidance, and practical assistance to ensure a smooth transition to motherhood.

Join me in my classes or take advantage of my doula services, and let's embark on this wonderful journey together!

Baby Yoga Classes


Benefits of Baby Yoga

  • Enhanced Bonding between parent and baby
  • Improved Sleep Patterns for babies
  • Developmental Stimulation for motor skills
  • Relief from Discomfort such as colic

Join our baby yoga classes to experience these wonderful benefits with your little one.

Doula Services


Benefits of Doula Follow-Up

  • Emotional Support during postpartum period
  • Assistance with Breastfeeding
  • Postpartum Recovery Guidance
  • Household Support for new mothers

Our doula services aim to provide caring support to new mothers during the critical postpartum period.
